Stable

The unit of currency transacted on the Mason network is the Mason dollar. The value of a Mason dollar is pegged to the USD. You can cash out your Mason dollars at any time for good old fashioned cash.

Inclusive

Since the value of Mason Money is fixed there's no chance of becoming a millionaire overnight. The focus of Mason Money is to provide a currency that anyone can use regardless of who they are or where they come from.

Simple

Mason Money was designed to be a simple as possible. Mason Money addresses look like email addresses (eg. alice$mason.money). Mason money is also developer friendly.

Green

The Mason Money network is much more energy efficient than the Bitcoin network. Instead of constantly mining every transaction that goes onto the network miners mine for "shares" of the network. Once a share is mined that miner no longer needs to expend energy to keep the network secure.
